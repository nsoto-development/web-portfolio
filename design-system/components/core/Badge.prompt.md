Small pill label for status, roles, or counts — not clickable (see `Tag` for clickable/removable chips).

```jsx
<Badge tone="success" dot>Live</Badge>
<Badge tone="neutral">v2.1</Badge>
<Badge tone="brand">Featured</Badge>
```

Tones: `brand`, `neutral` (default), `success`, `warning`, `danger`. `dot` prepends a small status dot.
